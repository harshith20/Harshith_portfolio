About Me

Hello, my name is Harshith and I graduated from NIT Warangal in 2021. My interest in data science was sparked when I learned about Posco, one of the biggest steel manufacturing companies, implementing artificial intelligence to reduce costs and greenhouse gases, and achieve net-zero carbon emissions. Since then, I have been working on personal projects in the field of data science and machine learning.

My previous work experience as a software engineer has equipped me with strong technical skills, and now I am eagerly seeking a role in the data-related field to further enhance my knowledge and expertise.

I am passionate about using data science to solve real-world problems and contribute to building a sustainable future.
